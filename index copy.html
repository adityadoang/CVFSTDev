What is CIVFEST?

Civil Engineering Festival (Civfest) is an annual event organized by HMS PNJ. This event is a forum for collaboration, innovation, and creativity for students, and helps them develop themselves through various activities related to civil engineering issues discussed. Presented as a national competition and national seminar, Civfest focuses on learning, innovation, and contribution. This event is a forum for civil engineering activists and solutions to overcome future challenges.

Vision & Mission

Our commitment to advancing student development and infrastructure resilience.

Our Vision

Realizing Civfest as a superior exploration platform utilizing the role and potential of students in Civil Engineering. We aim to increase awareness through innovation and creativity to create resilient infrastructure for the future.

Our Mission

  1. Organizing educational and competitive activities designed to improve the understanding, skills, and creativity of students and college students in the field of Civil Engineering.
  2. Implementing ideas and innovations produced by participants in real forms that are sustainable, relevant, and provide positive contributions to the development of resilient infrastructure in the future.
  3. Strengthening the relationship between participants, organizers, industry parties and sponsors who support the event.
