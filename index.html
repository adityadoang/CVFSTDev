What is CIVFEST?

Civil Engineering Festival (Civfest) is an annual event organized by HMS PNJ. This event is a forum for collaboration, innovation, and creativity for students, and helps them develop themselves through various activities related to civil engineering issues discussed. Presented as a national competition and national seminar, Civfest focuses on learning, innovation, and contribution. This event is a forum for civil engineering activists and solutions to overcome future challenges.

Vision & Mission

Our commitment to advancing student development and infrastructure resilience.

Our Vision

Maximizing CIVFEST as a premier platform for exploration, role development, and optimizing the potential of the youth in Civil Engineering through continuous innovation and creativity, to increase awareness and tangible contributions in realizing sustainable and green infrastructure for the future.

Our Mission

  1. Organizing educational and competitive activities to enhance the understanding, skills, and creativity of students in the field of Civil Engineering.
  2. Encouraging the creation and implementation of sustainable ideas and innovations that are applicable and relevant to the development of resilient and green infrastructure in the future.
  3. Strengthening collaboration among students, academics, and event partners to create a positive synergy.

National BIM Competition

The National BIM Competition (NBC) is a national-scale competition open to active undergraduate students in Civil Engineering programs across Indonesia. It serves as a platform for participants to channel their creativity and innovation in designing building structures using Building Information Modeling (BIM) technology.

Theme: "Green Building Design through BIM for a Sustainable Future"

National Concrete Competition

The National Concrete Competition (NCC) is a competition that provides Civil Engineering students with a platform to develop their creativity, innovation, and efficiency in designing high-strength concrete mixtures. Participants are challenged to enhance the mechanical properties of concrete by incorporating recycled materials into the mix design.

Theme: "Exploration of Recycled Alternative Materials as Concrete Components"

National Building Mockup Design

The National Building Mockup Design Competition (NBMDC) is one of the competition events held by the Civil Engineering Festival 2026 (Civfest 2026), specifically open to Senior High School (SMA/SMK) students and equivalents. It serves as a platform for participants to develop and express their ideas and creativity in constructing a small-scale house model that showcases Architectural Design, with a strong emphasis on Green Building innovation as a response to the challenges of the future.

Theme: “Green Building Design for Comfortable Future”
